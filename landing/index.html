Features

Everything you need for 3-phase analysis

Built for electrical engineering students and professionals who need quick, accurate circuit calculations.

Interactive Diagram

Tap directly on circuit components to enter values. The SVG diagram updates in real time as you configure your circuit.

Phasor Diagrams

Visualize voltage and current phasors with an interactive, zoomable diagram. Tap any phasor to see its exact value.

Full Power Analysis

Get per-phase and total apparent, real, and reactive power with power factor for every calculation.

Balanced & Unbalanced

Toggle between balanced and unbalanced systems. Balanced mode auto-fills all phases from a single input.

How It Works

Three steps to results

No complex setup. Just pick, tap, and calculate.

Pick a Configuration

Choose from Y-Y, Y-Delta, Delta-Y, or Delta-Delta. Set balanced or unbalanced, and select your phase sequence.

Enter Values

Tap any component on the circuit diagram to open the input modal. Enter values in rectangular or phasor form.

Get Results

Press Calculate to see all voltages, currents, power values, and an interactive phasor diagram.
